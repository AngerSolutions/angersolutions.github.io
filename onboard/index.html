1. Download fillable onboarding PDF

You can use a program like Adobe Reader , Firefox browser , Edge browser, Mac Preview , FoxIt Reader , or Adobe Fill & Sign (mobile) to fill in all of the fields. Then use their signature or pen annotation tool to sign each completed page.

Alternatively there are online cloud services (which may or may not require sign-up) such as Acrobat Online and DocFly which don't require you to install an app.
